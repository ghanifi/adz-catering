Frequently Asked

How quickly can you set up a cafeteria on our site?

Depending on the type of solution required, we can typically have a mobile food truck operational within days and a full cafeteria installation ready within 1–2 weeks of confirmation. We work around your site schedule to minimise disruption.

What size sites do you cater for?

We cater for sites of all sizes — from small teams of 20 to large developments with hundreds of workers. Our flexible range of solutions (mobile trucks, food cabins, vending, full cafeterias) means we can find the right fit for any project.

Do you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes — our menu includes vegetarian options, and we are happy to discuss specific dietary needs or allergy requirements with our clients. We aim to provide a menu that works for your entire workforce.

Are you fully compliant with UK food safety regulations?

Absolutely. We hold all necessary food safety certifications, maintain the highest hygiene standards, and ensure all our staff are trained in food safety practices. We are fully compliant with all relevant UK legislation.

Can you provide catering for the entire duration of a long project?

Yes — we specialise in long-term site catering partnerships. Our projects with Countryside and Bovis Homes demonstrate our ability to reliably serve a workforce for the full duration of a major construction project, adapting as the team's needs change.

How do I get a quote?

Simply fill in the contact form above, email us, or call us directly. We'll ask you a few questions about your site, workforce size, and what you need — then put together a tailored proposal at no obligation.
