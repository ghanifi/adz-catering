About Ad'z Catering

Our Vision

A New Standard in Site Catering

At Ad'z Catering Limited, our vision is to boost energy levels, the well-being, and efficiency of construction workers by providing access to healthy, nutritious, and high-quality meals.

Our goal is to offer catering services that set a new standard in the construction sector by delivering tailored solutions that have quality, hygiene, and satisfaction in mind.

By continuously improving our services and maintaining our dedication to the industry, we see ourselves as the leading brand shaping not only the catering services of today but also those of the future.

Who We Are

More Than a Meal

We believe that a well-fed workforce is a motivated, productive workforce. That's why we go beyond simply providing food — we create an experience that workers look forward to every day.

From our first project to our most recent, we have built our reputation on three pillars: quality, reliability, and a genuine understanding of the construction industry.

We work directly with site managers, contractors, and developers to ensure our catering solutions integrate seamlessly into the workflow — never disrupting, always delivering.

Track Record

Proven on the Ground

At Ad'z Catering Limited, we have successfully completed and continue to work on many significant projects in the construction sector, providing catering services, kitchen solutions, and mobile food infrastructure.

New Market Countryside

Partner: Countryside

As part of the New Market Sites project, we worked alongside Countryside and provided services using a 20ft food container. The container was specifically designed and installed to meet the meal requirements of the workers and adapted to the needs of the project.

02
Completed

Elsenham — Bovis Homes

Partner: Bovis Homes

In collaboration with Bovis Homes, we operated with our 16ft food container on the Elsenham project. Despite the limited space on the construction site, the container was efficiently used to provide high-quality meal services to the workers.
